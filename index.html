About Me

I understand your problem. Then I build the solution.

Jacob is an engineer at Microsoft where he builds systems that Fortune 500 companies — including some of the world's largest financial institutions and AI labs — depend on to run without failure. For over five years, his job has been to take complex, ambiguous problems, understand them deeply, and ship solutions that work reliably at scale. He knows what it takes to go from "we have a problem" to "here's something that actually fixes it."

Outside of that, Jacob independently builds and ships AI-powered products — tools that cut hours of manual work down to minutes, automate repetitive processes, and give small teams capabilities they couldn't afford to build otherwise. Every project on this site started as a real problem and ended as a working solution.

Through Bedrock Technical Solutions, Jacob works with business owners and small teams to find where AI can make the biggest difference — then builds it. You don't need to understand the technology. You just need to know what's taking too long, costing too much, or holding your team back. That's enough to start the conversation.
